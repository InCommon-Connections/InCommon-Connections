Meet the Founder

Following a passion for human connection.

Edith founded InCommon Connections to follow her passion for human connection and the power of gratitude — and to help mission-driven organizations bring people together in ways that truly matter.

"I believe the best gatherings do more than run smoothly. They leave people feeling seen, connected, and ready to do meaningful work together."

With more than two decades of leadership experience, she provides personalized, collaborative support to nonprofits, educational institutions, and healthcare organizations — pairing deep operational competence with warmth and authenticity at every step.
